About Nova Web Studio

Built from passion.
Driven by purpose.

Hi, I'm Michelle — the founder and designer behind Nova Web Studio. I'm a remote web designer and developer serving small businesses across the Netherlands and UK from Oostvoorne, South Holland.

I trained at SheCodes with certifications in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Python and API integration. I believe every small business deserves a professional website — not just a digital brochure, but a tool that genuinely brings in new clients.

I work with beauty salons, tradespeople, health practitioners, childcare providers, and any small business ready to get serious about their online presence.
