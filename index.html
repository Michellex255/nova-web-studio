About Nova Web Studio

One person.
Your business.

Nova Web Studio is a boutique web design studio based in Oostvoorne, South Holland. When you work with us, you get one dedicated person who learns your business, understands your clients, and builds something that actually works for you — not a generic template handed off by a junior developer.

No agency overheads. No AI shortcuts. No cookie-cutter designs. Every website is hand-built from scratch, designed to convert visitors into customers and reflect your business with the quality it deserves.

Nova Web Studio serves small businesses across the Netherlands and UK — from independent retailers and service providers to health practitioners and trades. If you want a website that actually grows your business, you're in the right place.
