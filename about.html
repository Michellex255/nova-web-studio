The honest version

Not the LinkedIn version.
The real one.

Nova Web Studio exists because I couldn't find what I was looking for — a web designer who actually listens, builds something personal, and doesn't disappear after the invoice. So I became that person instead.

I'm based in Oostvoorne, in the heart of Voorne-Putten. I walk the same streets as my clients, I know the businesses around here, and I understand what it means to build something from nothing in a place where nobody hands you anything.

I didn't come from a design school or a big agency. I built this through stubbornness, curiosity, and a refusal to settle for less than what I know is possible.

I'm SheCodes certified — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Python, and API integration. I also serve clients in the UK, which means I understand both markets and both mentalities. And yes, I speak Dutch.

How I work

You talk.
I actually listen.

I don't start designing until I understand your business. Not the surface version — the real version. What makes you different, who your customers actually are, what you're proud of and what keeps you up at night.

Every project is a conversation, not a transaction. You'll always know what's being built, why, and when. No jargon, no disappearing acts, no surprises on the invoice.

And when I hand over your website, I don't vanish. The Care Plan means I'm still there — updating, maintaining, answering questions — because a website isn't finished when it goes live. It's finished when it stops working for you. Which, if I do my job right, is never.
